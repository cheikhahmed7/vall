Q. What services does Golden Diligent International Agency provide?

Golden Diligent International Agency specializes in a range of services, including education Counseling, university guidance, visa assistance, course selection, immigration support, English exams, and IELTS classes, as well as consultancy for couple visas.

Our mission is to help students develop professionally for competitive employment in the global workforce.

Q. How can I apply for a student visa through Golden Diligent?

Applying for a student visa with Golden Diligent is a straightforward process. First, engage in a consultation with our experienced advisors to determine the best course and destination for your academic goals.

Once the course is chosen, our team will guide you through the necessary documentation, assist with the visa application form, and provide support throughout the process. We aim to make the visa application journey smooth and stress-free.

Q. What English proficiency exams do you offer preparation for?

Golden Diligent offers comprehensive preparation for a variety of English proficiency exams, with a particular focus on the International English Language Testing System (IELTS).

Our classes cover all sections of the IELTS exam, including listening, reading, writing, and speaking. Our experienced instructors tailor the preparation to your specific needs, ensuring you are well-prepared for success.

Q. How can I schedule a virtual consultation with a Golden Diligent advisor?

Scheduling a virtual consultation with one of our advisors is simple. Visit our website and navigate to the "Contact Us" page. Fill out the consultation request form, indicating your preferred date and time.

Our team will get in touch with you to confirm the appointment details and ensure you have all the necessary information for a productive consultation.

Q. What sets Golden Diligent apart from other study abroad agencies?

Golden Diligent stands out for its personalized approach, experienced team of advisors, and a comprehensive range of services. We prioritize individualized guidance, ensuring that each client's unique needs and goals are met.

Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and staying abreast of industry trends sets us apart as a trusted partner in the pursuit of global education and career opportunities.
